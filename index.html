Hope

By: Jared Wullschleger

"Hope, when you feel tied up with a rope,
But you have a feeling that
You'll be out in no time flat."

"When you feel chained up in Hell,
But you know it'll be all well."

"When you feel that life has gone to trash
And everything just happened in a flash."

"But with all that stress,
You will be able to rest."

"And you don't know what to do or say."
"But life will get better someday."
